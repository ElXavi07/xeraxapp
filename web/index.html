Boot-image troubleshooting

Paste the exact extractor, ADB, fastboot, or Magisk error. Xerax matches reviewed failure patterns and will not execute suggested commands.

Automation with hard stops.

Xerax refuses ambiguous device states, multiple connected devices, locked bootloaders, invalid images, and unconfirmed serial numbers.

  • Local-only ADB and fastboot execution
  • Exact commands shown in the activity log
  • No AI-generated commands or silent fallbacks
  • Flashing disabled by default in the companion
